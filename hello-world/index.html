## It's so good!!! Make bubble milk tea at home

With the simplest ingredients, restore the most pure taste

step1 Prepare the ingredients:

  • Glutinous rice flour
  • The tea
  • milk
  • White granulated sugar
  • Brown sugar

step2 The production process:

The whole process is divided into two parts:

  1. To make the pearl
  2. Making tea with milk

I.To make the pearl

1.Melt the brown sugar in hot water and prepare 2 small bowls
2.Pour the glutinous rice flour into a container, then pour 1 bowl of brown sugar water
3.Knead into a large ball
4.Knead again into a small ball, need to be wrapped in dry glutinous rice flour outside
5.Put the second bowl of brown sugar water into the pot, boil open into the knead good ball, keep scratching, to prevent sticking to the bottom of the pot, scratching until the sticky wire can be loaded

II.Making tea with milk

1.Pour the milk into the pan and bring to the boil
2.Add the tea leaves to the boiled milk and cook until it turns to a milky tea color
3.Put it in the prepared cup while it is hot
4.Pour in the boiled pearls

Dang dang! Good and simple pearl milk tea is ready!!
